E.F.E.C.A.

Enemy Floating Energy Calsulator for Axie


Enemy Floating Energy calculator for Axie or also known as E.F.E.C.A. is an android app which assist players in calculating enemy's energy. E.F.E.C.A. can be used while playing Axie Infinity on your android device. It aims to lessen or eliminate the gap of player’s skill level between novice and seasoned gamers in Axie Infinity.

This is a fan made app by ‘Dy ng Pinas’ and not associated with Axie Infinity or Sky Mavis.
